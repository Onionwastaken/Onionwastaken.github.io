Coleen's German Potato Salad

Ingredients

  • 12 slices of bacon, fried and crumbled
  • 12 potatoes, boiled to done with a generous amount of salt
  • 5 stalks of celery, chopped
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 2 cups oatmeal
  • 1 cup shaved coconut

Method

  1. Mix butter, sugar, cocoa, and milk in a saucepan, bringing the mixture to a boil.
  2. Let boil for one minute and remove from heat.
  3. Add peanut butter, oatmeal, and shaved coconut. Mix until combined.
  4. Spoon onto tinfoil to cool.

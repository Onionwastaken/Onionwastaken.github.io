You make your way into the tunnels... alone.

A sharp chill runs down your spine as you pull a torch from your pack. Lighting the torch brings much needed warmth to your hands. Slowly, you delve deeper into the tunnel. You have only taken a few steps, when suddenly...

SLAM!

A kobold jumps down from the ceiling, ripping deep gashes into your chest. You are doing everything you can to cling to your life, but you are losing blood, quickly. The kobold rummages through your bag, and as you lose conciousness, you can see him hold up your silver locket.

Maybe you should've been a shepherd like dad.

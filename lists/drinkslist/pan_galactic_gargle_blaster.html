Pan Galactic Gargle Blaster

Ingredients

  • 1 bottle Ol' Janx Spirit
  • 1 measure of water from the seas of Santraginus V
  • 3 cubes of Arcturian Mega-gin
  • 4 litres of Fallian marsh gas
  • 1 measure of Qualactin Hypermint extract
  • The tooth of an Algolian Suntiger
  • Zamphuor to taste
  • 1 olive

Method

  1. In a fairly sized glass, pour in Ol' Janx Spirit and Santraginean water over Mega-gin cubes.
  2. Take a moment of silence as you filter the marsh gas through, remembering those who died of pleasure in those Fallian marshes.
  3. Over the back of a silver spoon(the silver being imperative), add Hypermint.
  4. Drop in Suntiger tooth.
  5. Add olive and sprinkle Zamphuor.
  6. Drink carefully. I mean it when I say carefully.

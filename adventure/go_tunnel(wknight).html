The two of you stand before the fetid gates.

The stale air fills your lungs. A black veil hides the interior of the large pipe. In the distance, you can hear echoes, but only faintly. You are grateful for Maximan's company.

"Lo, squire. There seems to be something nearby..."

You watch as Maximan leads his horse into the tunnel... he steps down and lights a torch...

SLAM!

A slender kobold drops from the tunnel roof, dropping on top of Maximan. As you rush in to help your knight right himself, a rather burly looking kobold steps between the two of you.

He raises his axe, and you make a split second descision.


Slide underneath him and counterattack. Dodge nimbly to the side and plant his axe firmly in the ground.
